# **ADM 1 – Financial Accounting**

*Recording, classifying, and reporting financial transactions.*

* **ADM 1.1 – Journal Entry Recording**
  Records financial transactions; consists of debit/credit ledger entries.

* **ADM 1.2 – General Ledger Maintenance**
  Maintains account balances; consists of account classification and postings.

* **ADM 1.3 – Financial Statement Preparation**
  Generates income statement, balance sheet, cash flow; consists of aggregated financial data.

* **ADM 1.4 – Period Closing**
  Closes accounting period; consists of reconciliation and closing entries.

* **ADM 1.5 – Account Reconciliation**
  Matches ledger balances to supporting documents; consists of variance adjustment entries.
