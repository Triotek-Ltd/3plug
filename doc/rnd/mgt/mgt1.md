# **Strategic & Control Engine**

# **MGT 1 – Strategic Planning**

*Defining long-term direction, objectives, and strategic initiatives.*

* **MGT 1.1 – Vision & Mission Definition**
  Establishes organizational purpose; consists of approved vision/mission statements.

* **MGT 1.2 – Strategic Objective Setting**
  Defines long-term goals; consists of measurable strategic targets.

* **MGT 1.3 – Strategic Initiative Planning**
  Identifies key programs; consists of initiative charters and timelines.

* **MGT 1.4 – Strategy Alignment Mapping**
  Links objectives to departments; consists of cross-functional alignment records.

* **MGT 1.5 – Strategy Review & Revision**
  Evaluates strategy effectiveness; consists of adjustment approvals and version updates.
