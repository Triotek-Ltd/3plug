# **OPS 1 – Process Design & Mapping**

*Designing and governing how operational work is structured and controlled.*

* **OPS 1.1 – Process Identification**
  Defines operational processes that exist; consists of cataloging workflows and assigning ownership records.

* **OPS 1.2 – Process Mapping (As-Is / To-Be)**
  Documents workflow structure; consists of step sequencing, inputs/outputs, and role linkage.

* **OPS 1.3 – SOP Creation & Documentation**
  Establishes formal procedures; consists of structured instruction records and compliance controls.

* **OPS 1.4 – Workflow Rule Definition**
  Defines decision logic; consists of trigger conditions and routing parameters.

* **OPS 1.5 – Role & Responsibility Mapping (RACI)**
  Assigns accountability; consists of responsibility matrix records.

* **OPS 1.6 – Control Point Definition**
  Inserts validation gates; consists of approval checkpoints and audit markers.

* **OPS 1.7 – KPI Definition**
  Establishes measurable performance indicators; consists of metric definitions and thresholds.

* **OPS 1.8 – Process Version Control**
  Manages revisions; consists of change logs and approval history.

* **OPS 1.9 – Process Change Management**
  Controls process modifications; consists of change request submission and approval transactions.

* **OPS 1.10 – Process Retirement**
  Deactivates obsolete workflows; consists of archival and status updates.
